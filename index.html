About

Turning research into real-world AI

Wawan is a seasoned AI and data science leader with over 12 years of experience across AI, data science, and software engineering. He has led numerous AI/Data Science projects to production, with applications spanning e-commerce, agriculture, and healthcare.

As a researcher, he has authored 90+ peer-reviewed international publications and serves as a reviewer for prestigious journals including Nature Scientific Reports, IEEE Access, IEEE JSTARS, and PLOS ONE. He also holds four copyrights for AI-based video and image analytics software.

He has been an NVIDIA-Certified Deep Learning Institute Instructor since 2017, empowering the next generation of AI practitioners through hands-on teaching at BINUS and beyond.
